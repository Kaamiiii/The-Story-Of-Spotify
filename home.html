The Story of Spotify

- Home

Spotify was founded by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon as a way to deal with the problem of music piracy. Before the music streaming services got popular, there were many who downloaded pirated music files. This was a growing challenge for the whole music industry and is the base of Spotify history.

Spotify Technology net worth as of May 06, 2022 is $21.41B. Spotify Technology S.A. provides music streaming services.

The CEO of Spotify is still to this day, Daniel Ek.

Spotify was developed in 2006 by the Spotify AB team in Stockholm, Sweden. The company was founded by Daniel Ek, former technical director of Stardoll, and Martin Lorentzon, co-founder of TradeDoubler. Spotify AB is a subsidiary of Spotify Ltd., based in London, UK, which in turn is part of the holding company Spotify Technology S.A. domiciled in Luxembourg.

In November 2011, Spotify introduced the Spotify Apps service which made it possible for third-party developers to contribute HTML5 applications that could be hosted within the Spotify player desktop. The apps provided featured features like synced lyrics, song reviews, and curated playlists.
